About GLASENSE

​Welcome to GLASense, the pioneering team representing the University of Glasgow in the SensUs competition. We are a spirited cohort of interdisciplinary students, passionately driven to redefine the landscape of sensory technology. Our diverse expertise in fields like technology, innovation, and design converges to fuel our collective ambition. Committed to excellence and fuelled by innovation, we're on a mission to revolutionise sensory technology. Through collaborative ingenuity and academic rigor, we're crafting ground-breaking solutions that transcend boundaries.

Join us on this exhilarating journey as we merge academic prowess with relentless curiosity to reshape the future of sensory technology and unlock new possibilities!
