About GLASENSE

GlaSense is the University of Glasgow’s dedicated team competing in SensUs 2025, an international competition focused on developing innovative biosensors for healthcare. Our team brings together passionate students from engineering, science, and design to tackle this year’s challenge. 

With a strong track record of award-winning solutions, we are excited to push the boundaries of biosensing technology. Competing against 17 universities worldwide, we will showcase our sensor at the SensUs Innovation Days in August, contributing to the future of healthcare innovation. If you are interested in taking part in this challenge, please reach out to us!
